Carbon Emission Budget Calculator

Below application is an extended interactive version of a blogpost from Emission Budget blogpost of Stefan Rahmstorf. Currently it displays historical country emissions for all countries in the world, calculates national and personal carbon budgets based on a given total global carbon budget.
