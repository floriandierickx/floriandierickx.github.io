EU ETS & Carbon Leakage List

Documentation related to the EU Emission Trading Scheme, compiled in the context of an academic position paper I wrote in 2017 for the revision of the ETS System & Carbon Leakage List 2021 - 2030. A summary of the consultation process, functioning of the EU ETS and a case study on how EU ETS funds are used in Flanders can be found on Twitter or in this blogpost.
